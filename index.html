Welcome to Spaghetti & Cruft

Feeding Geeks Since 1999

The Team

At Spaghetti & Cruft, both our cooks and wait staff do their utmost to provide the greatest customer service possible. Our pizzas are all hand crafted on the spot, using but the best ingredients. Our pizzas are baked to perfection in a rustic wood-fired brick oven.

Our Vision

Spaghetti & Cruft has a very simple business model -- to appeal to geeks alike and bring the booming community of developers and designers together over mouth watering, delicious pizza. In the future, we plan to expand our current restaraunt into a more robust workshop; including both laptops and computers for convenient usage and special rooms for numerous events.

Who We Are

Recognizing the demand for good food at fair prices, brothers, Mario and Salvador, opened a neighborhood pizzeria in Gotham, California in 1999. Business began very good, but things really took off when the brothers had the brilliant notion of catering to the neighborhood geeks, the designers and the developers busily building new pieces of the World Wide Web. The brothers brought in comfy couches to complement the red checkered tablecloths, installed an array of wireless routers to provide free boradband connectivity for their customers, and even tracked down a vintage Asteroids arcade machine. They soon after then reopened their eatery namely known as Mario and Sal's Pizzeria to the now renown Spaghetti and Cruft Geek Pizzeria.

Behind Our Doors

Behind our pizzaria's doors, you'll find countless forms of entertainment. We hold weekly events, hackathons, and much more! Waiting for your order? Play some of our retro arcade games such as Asteroids.
